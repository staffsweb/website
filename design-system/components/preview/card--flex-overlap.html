Add a class of card--flex-overlap to have a card's figure and content size themselves intrinsically with flexbox, when using a highlighted title style. The title is overlapped on the figure (when the figure sits above). This is particularly useful for cards in single columns (like search results), and grids of 3, which look awkwardly large otherwise at a tablet-ish width, when they fall to a single column.
